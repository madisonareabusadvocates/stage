An Independent Citizen Voice for Buses

Welcome to the home page of Madison Area Bus Advocates. We are a diverse group of bus users and friends who aim to inform people about the reality and the possibilities of a Madison Metro Transit, to encourage system development, and to encourage bus ridership. We believe that good public transit supports the dignity and freedom of all individuals, creates an open, hospitable and welcoming community, respects the environment, opens economic opportunity to all and moves people to where they need to go. Join Us!

NEWS: The Madison Area has a Regional Transit Authority that hasagreed to referendum approval before enacting up to a .05% sales tax. Meetings are listed with the Dane County Clerk. For more information, see the unofficial RTA Coalition site DaneTransit.org.

More up to date news and discussion can be had by joining our Yahoo! listserve at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/mabaaa/.

Opinion: Regional Transport Authority (RTA)

The R in RTA stands for "regional," not "rail." The first priorities of an RTA here should be in establishing and operating a centrally-located terminal for inter-city and intra-city transit, in enhancing our existing bus transit system within Madison, and in adding rapid buses that can compete with the automobile on travel-time and convenience and that can better connect communities through the Madison Area.
