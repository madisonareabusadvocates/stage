BRT Plan: Metro Mover "The M"

Our Rapid Bus (BRT) plan consists of BRT vehicles operating over these corridors and extending into the entire urbanized area. The runningway is generally on arterial streets with transit priority elements. Model systems include Kansas City Max, Las Vegas Max, Eugene Emerald Express, and Los Angeles MetroRapid. Service is frequent and all-day, with branded vehicles and premium stations spaced at one-third to one mile. The system is designed to incorporate future conversion to light rail.
