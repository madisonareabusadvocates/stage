Ted Voth Jr.: Only an hour to plan route for holiday bus ride!

The Capital Times, December 29, 2010

Dear Editor: What a user-friendly, perspicacious delight is Madison Metro's Ride Guide! It took me less than an hour to plan my route from Willy Street to my friend's house for Christmas dinner!

And it wasn't at all confusing trying to remember that the No. 3 doesn't run on weekends or holidays, only the No. 7.

Why, it took me less than 15 minutes alone to find the feeder buses running out of the West Transfer Point.

No wonder folks are clamoring to ride Metro.

Ted Voth Jr.
Madison
