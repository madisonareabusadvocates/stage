Bill McCormick: Bus drivers do great job in snowstorms

The Capital Times, Mon. Dec. 29, 2008
Bill McCormick

Dear Editor:

I would like to thank all the bus drivers in Madison for the great service we got during the snowstorms. I have been riding the city bus since 1990 and they are always nice to me.

If they raise the bus fare, I would be OK with it because maybe the bus drivers deserve a raise too.

Bill McCormick
Madison
