Bus ads unsightly, inside and out

Wisconsin State Journal
OPINION

November 8, 2007
David Weast, Madison People angered by the awful mega-advertisements blanketing Metro Transit buses might consider the view from the inside as well. Riding the bus used to mean sitting back and watching the world stream by the window. Now it feels like peering through the fabric of an army tent.
