BEER, CASINO ADS ON BUSES SEND WRONG MESSAGE

The Capital Times
EDITORIAL :: A9

August 16, 2007
Sally Dreher, Madison Dear Editor: What are our city officials thinking? To advertise beer and casinos on Metro buses, although they said they won't be used on school routes! Do they not think our students see buses throughout the city? We have enough vices in this city, so why so boldly advertise gambling and drinking?
