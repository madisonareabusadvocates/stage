Beer bus wrap is appalling

The Cap Times
August 15, 2012

Dear Editor: I couldnt believe the beer bus that came this morning while I was waiting on Jenifer Street for the next bus downtown running as a Route 10, bus number #015 was a fully wrapped bus with an advertisement for Pabst Blue Ribbon Beer consisting of giant beer cans stacked up one on another covering every inch of the surface of the bus, each can so large that it only took six of them to spread across the width of the front of the bus. The wrapping even included the entrance doors of the bus, so passengers would have to have the beer cans in our face while we wait for the doors to open so that we can board the bus!!!

I thought the Metro Transit administration had assured the citizens of Madison that with them assuming management of the bus wrap advertising program that this type of undesirable message would not happen again?

Appalled and aghast,

Laurie Wermter
Madison
