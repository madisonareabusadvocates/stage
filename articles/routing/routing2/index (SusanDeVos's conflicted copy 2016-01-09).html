Routing and Levels of Service

Madison Area Bus Advocates's Strategic Plan calls for at least 6 different types of bus routes or service:

            1. core service running almost all the time
            2. direct commuter service to and from outside Madison
            3. peak commuter service with limited stops within Madison
            4. circulators and shuttles
            5. service for special need populations
            6. connectors to intercity transport carriers

Rerouting in the Fall of 2006

Metro Transit's restructuring undermined welcome innovations with undue emphasis on cuts. Metro Transit called the rerouting "improvements" and "efficiences" but failed to also say that those changes often involved major service cuts and continued a trend of declining bus service. Metro held a Public Hearing May 22, 2006 regarding the changes. Many thought the outcome a foregone conclusion.
