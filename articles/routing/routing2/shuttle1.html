Metro Transit Will Start Shuttle Service

Wisconsin State Journal :: LOCAL :: B3

Metro Transit, in partnership with the Madison Parking Utility, will begin a Parking Ramp Shuttle Service this month.

The service, which begins Tuesday and is part of a five-month pilot program, will offer free rides between Downtown and campus parking ramps for any drivers parked at these ramps.

City officials hope the shuttle service will alleviate crowding in some ramps by making it easier to park in other, underused ramps.
