BUS HUB

The Capital Times, July 9, 1997

As a resident of tne Hm Farms neighborhood, I would like to say that I was shocked by the recent terrible beating of a man for no apparent reason at Rennebomn Park. However, I would like to say that although this appears to be an isolated incident, I fear for what is going to happen to our neighborhood and this is probably just a harbinger of things to come when the bus hub is put' into operation on Sheboygan Avenue, which will bring in strangers from all parts of the city to our once quiet and safe neighborhood."
