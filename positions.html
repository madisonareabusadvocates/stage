Positions

Resolutions

A collection of position statements and resolutions on transit equity, fare policies, service improvements, and transportation planning issues in the Madison area and beyond.

Transit Riders Manifesto

A comprehensive statement of principles advocating for public transportation as a basic public service, including fare-free transit, 24/7 core service, and rider input in transit planning.

Strategic Plan

Our vision for a sustainable, fiscally-sound, inclusive, improved, and expanded public transit system throughout the Greater Madison Area, focusing on regional transit authority, multimodal terminals, and transit-friendly development.
