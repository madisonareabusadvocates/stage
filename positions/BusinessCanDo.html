Top 5 Things Local Business Can Do

 

  1. Know where the nearest operating bus stop is and be ready to tell someone when asked;

  2. Provide schedule and route information for buses that go near your place; (some such as the 38 only run Monday through Friday during the morning and afternoon rush hours);

  3. Make bus passes a worker benefit; (Use the federal Commuter Choice program and lobby State representatives for a State program as well); for more info see Commuter Choice.

  4. Become a sales outlet for Metro to help make passes available; for more info see Become An Outlet.

  5. Join Madison Area Bus Advocates and proudly display your membership;
