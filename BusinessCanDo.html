Top 4 Things Businesses Can Do

  1. Provide patrons with up-to-date bus stop, schedule and route information when asked. It is important that the information by up-to-date because changes are made constantly.

  2. Put walking, bicycling, and bus directions on your website. Directions should not be limited to car driving and car parking.

  3. Show the community you care by being a visible sales outlet. For information on being a Metro sales outlet go to www.cityofmadison.com/Metro/fares/salesoutlets/becomeoutlet.cfm.

  4. Join Madison Area Bus Advocates in our effort to improve the bus as a safe, efficient, clean, convenient, and affordable means of travel throughout the greater Madison area. Encourage and empower others as well.
