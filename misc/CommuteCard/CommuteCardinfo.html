COMMUTE CARD PROGRAM: Starting in 2010

 

Join the Madison Credit Union and other

green-minded businesses in offering the Metro Commute Card.

Every organization has a daily agenda of keeping morale
at its best, enhancing ones corporate identity and trying to incorporate green business practices, all while trying to keep revenues intact.

Innovative thinking about affordable investments in company benefits, like the Metro Commute Card, can help achieve a number of these goals. Its good for the bottom line. Its good for the environment. Its good for all of us.

And best of all, the Commute Card isnt limited to just businesses. Non-profit groups, schools, colleges and technical campuses can all take advantage of the Commute Card program.

Benefits to your organization

Promotes greener living and your organization as environmentally responsible

Proven morale and productivity enhancer

Tax savings (According to Internal Revenue Code 26 USC 132 (f)

Attractive benefit for both new and current employees

Alleviates any on-site parking issues

Helps reduce transportation-related absences

Benefits to the Commute Card Holder
Reduces commuting costs Commute Card holders save on gas, tires, maintenance, parking, etc.

Reduces the stress and hassle of commuting (traffic congestion)

Individually helping to conserve the environment

Alleviates parking problems

The Metro Commute card can be a smart choice for every organization. Not only is it an opportunity to boost morale within your organization, it’s a big step in helping the environment and Madison’s efforts to go GREEN!

Join the following businesses that have already signed up and will start offering the Metro Commute Card in 2010.

Madison Credit Union, ITT Technical Institute, Interactive Media Solutions, Wisconsin Youth Symphony Orchestras, Broadcast Interactive Media, XNR Productions, L'Etoile Restaurant, Madison Children's Museum, The Towers Apartments, Anthology, TeachingBooks.net, River Alliance of Wisconsin, and Community Pharmacy.
