The program’s first hour will feature three presentations:

  1. Prof. Emeritus John Magnuson, Limnology & Zoology on what a changed climate means for Wisconsin;
  2. Prof. Jon Foley, Director of SAGE at the Gaylord Nelson Institute of Environmental Studies on the effects of climate change on biological systems and human health, and the need to address this problem;
  3. Representative Spencer Black on a bill that he will soon introduce in the Wisconsin Assembly to limit greenhouse gas emissions in Wisconsin.

During the second hour, the three speakers will respond to questions and comments from the audience. Physicians for Social Responsibility’s Dr. Jeff Patterson will moderate this response panel.
